Cat Master

One day I had a cat. Then the cat attacked ruining my newly purchased curtains. It was awful, and I couldn't believe Mr. Pickles would do such a thing. Many of my visitors told me that Mr. Pickles was evil, but I didn't believe them after all why would an orange cat be evil. It just didn't make sense like he's a cat what evil is there? However, I asked the cat, and he said "well yes I am quite evil, and it only took you five years to notice. It's all because you gave me that cursed name Mr. Pickles." Weird times...

Bread Panic

I ran out of bread yesterday. It was the morning I was too tired to realize the severity of my circumstances. I just said "It's okay I'll get bread at the store later today". I kept going about my day as per usual, but then I realized how will I have grilled cheese for lunch without any bread. Panic set in, the world turned blue, and the hostilities began. I rushed out of work while my boss tried giving me this pink paper. Not sure what that was about. When I got to the supermarket the bread was gone. So I went to the next and kept searching. Alas, no bread could be found. So I went to my boss's house breaking in taking this baguette I saw on the counter. It was so fresh, so beautiful... Let's just say I had an amazing grilled cheese after that.
