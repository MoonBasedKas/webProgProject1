About us

Who are we?

The Family Alliance for Mental Health Recovery (FAMHR) was founded by a group of parents whose children were diagnosed with serious mental illness (SMI) or with psychosis. It includes people who have been diagnosed with SMI or psychosis, their family members and friends, and allies. See our stories here.
